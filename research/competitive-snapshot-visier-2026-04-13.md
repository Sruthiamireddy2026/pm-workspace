# Competitive Snapshot: Visier
*Date: 2026-04-13*

---

**What they do:** Cloud-native people analytics platform. Aggregates HR, payroll, and workforce data from multiple systems to deliver pre-built and custom analytics for HR and business leaders.

**Who they target:** Mid-market to enterprise companies (1,000+ employees). Buyer is typically the CHRO or VP of People Analytics. Strong in industries with complex workforce management needs: financial services, healthcare, retail, tech.

**Top 3 differentiators:**
- **Depth of people analytics out of the box** — hundreds of pre-built workforce metrics and benchmarks without needing to build from scratch
- **Workforce benchmarking** — anonymized cross-customer data lets customers compare their metrics (turnover, pay equity, hiring velocity) against industry peers
- **Embedded analytics / OEM model** — sells to HCM vendors (e.g., SAP, Ceridian) who embed Visier in their platform, giving them broad distribution

**Pricing:** Not public. Enterprise contracts, typically six figures annually. Priced by employee headcount. Implementation services often bundled or required.

**One weakness:** Heavy dependency on implementation and onboarding — time-to-value is slow. Customers frequently cite the need for consultant support to configure data integrations and customize metrics, which makes self-serve adoption harder and increases total cost.
